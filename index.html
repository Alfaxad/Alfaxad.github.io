IBM Quantum Challenge - Fall 2021

Achievement: Advanced

UNICEF/Y4C U-Report Rapid Pro Hackathon

I was a part of a team that built Sema na Kiongozi wako(SKW). SKW is a messaging app that links Government leaders more closely to citizens. SKW actually enables citizens to report specific issues in their particular constituency or region, the queries are stored and give the government a specific issue to work on, it also enables more youth to participate in development activities. We won 3rd place during the hackathon and currently the app is in pilot tests phases in Songwe.
