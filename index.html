Hello there! I study mechanical & electrical systems engineering at KUAS.I focus on robotics, AI and Quantum computing .


Alfaxad is an engineering student with a mission to revolutionize Africa's technology by contributing to fields such as AI, robotics, quantum computing and computational neuroscience. He is an IBM Qiskit advocate and GDSC lead at KUAS. Alfaxad has contributed in quantum computing research and has won various hackathons contributing to open-source AI development. Currently, he is focusing on applications of AI and robotics in healthcare, agriculture and infrastructures. In his free time he plays video games, codes, reads a comic book or a sci-fi novel. In addition to that he is a fan of Calvin & Hobbes, favorite DC & Marvel characters: The Batman & Spiderman.
