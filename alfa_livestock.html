ALFA: Automated Livestock Farming Assistant

September 26, 2024

What we're building

We are creating ALFA: Automated Livestock Farming Assistant, a groundbreaking system that integrates solar-powered environmental controls and AI-powered health monitoring to revolutionize poultry farming in Tanzania. By using cutting-edge technology, our system helps regulate key environmental factors such as temperature and humidity, ensuring optimal living conditions for poultry, especially in regions with unpredictable climates.

The AI-powered health monitoring system detects early signs of disease and health issues in poultry, allowing farmers to respond quickly and efficiently. ALFA automates the tracking and monitoring of bird health, helping farmers manage their flocks more effectively. The system is powered by affordable solar panels, making it accessible even to small-scale farmers in rural areas who might have limited access to electricity.

As temperatures continue to rise and the risk of disease increases, there is a pressing need for innovative solutions that can help farmers manage these challenges sustainably. ALFA addresses these issues by providing a reliable, low-cost, and sustainable solution for small and large-scale poultry farmers alike.
